Best Anime of 2018

1. Attack on Titan Season 3

An intense anime with a gripping storyline and amazing animation. Follow Eren Yeager and his friends as they continue to battle the Titans.

2. My Hero Academia Season 3

Experience the adventures of Izuku Midoriya and his classmates as they train to become professional heroes in a world filled with superpowers.

3. Violet Evergarden

Follow the journey of Violet, a former soldier, as she seeks to understand the meaning of emotions and love in a beautifully animated world.
